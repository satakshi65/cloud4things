About Us

Accelerate Innovation, Elevate Efficiency

Background :

Founded in 2019, C4T provides a digital utility platform for energy, water & gas using IoT-enabled service applications to improve the efficiency of buildings and utilities.

C4T offers a Digital-based, cloud-native Smart Utility Platform designed for the efficient management of electricity, water, and gas utilities. The solution boasts a microservices-based architecture and an open API structure, facilitating large-scale deployments and integration with intricate multi-vendor utility environments. The system encompasses essential modules, including Head End System-HES, Meter Data Management-MDM, Network Management System-NMS, Customer Management, Billing, and a mobile application to ensure seamless utility operations. The prepaid and postpaid billing features are highly customizable and flexible, providing a truly agile customer experience. The protocol-agnostic framework of the system enables smooth integration with various on-field meters and IoT gateways, presenting clients with a flexible and scalable solution.

At Cloud4Things, our mission is to drive business growth through our cutting-edge Digital Utility Platform. We strive to provide efficient applications and services that adapt to the evolving needs of the industry. By embracing innovative technologies, we cater to Electricity, Water, and Gas Utilities, as well as multi-tenant buildings. Our goal is to empower businesses to achieve sustained success and operational efficiency.

Our vision is to drive business growth by providing a self-service Digital Utility and Rapid Application Development Platform. This platform leverages next-generation technologies to deliver efficient business applications and services, with a focus on Electricity, Water, and Gas Utilities, multi-tenant buildings, and industries.

Enabling business expansion through a self-service portal offering efficiency applications as products and services. Utilizing IoT, AI, and digitization platforms to cater to multi-tenant buildings, industries and utilities.

C4T provides a Digital-based, cloud-native Smart Utility Platform for managing electricity, water, and gas utilities.
